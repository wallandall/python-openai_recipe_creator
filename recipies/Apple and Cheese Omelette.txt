

Recipe Title: Apple and Cheese Omelette

Ingredients:
- 1 apple, peeled and diced
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup shredded cheese
- 1/4 cup diced onion

Instructions:

1. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and diced onion.

3. Pour the egg mixture into the pre-heated skillet and cook until the eggs are almost set.

4. Add the diced apple and shredded cheese to the top of the omelette.

5. Reduce heat to low and cover the skillet with a lid.

6. Cook until the cheese has melted and the eggs are fully cooked.

7. Serve hot and enjoy!
